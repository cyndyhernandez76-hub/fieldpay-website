The Platform

Two apps. One platform.

FieldPay connects the field and the office in one continuous flow — so the hours your supervisor captures at 6am are running through payroll by Friday.

📱
Field App

FieldPay

The mobile time, attendance, and data collection app for crew leaders, supervisors, and field managers. Capture every hour, every piece, every assignment — right from your phone, right from the field.

  • Clock-in / clock-out with crew rosters
  • Piece-rate and production tracking
  • Crew, farm, and task assignment
  • GPS-stamped entries for compliance
  • Works offline — syncs when connected
💼
Back Office

ERP Suite

The full HRIS and ERP platform that powers the back office — from worker onboarding to payroll, from H-2A petition tracking to farm invoicing. Purpose-built for multi-state ag operations.

  • HRIS & worker lifecycle management
  • Payroll, AEWR & multi-state tax compliance
  • H-2A petition tracking & housing registry
  • Farm invoicing across all billing models
  • Financial automation & bank reconciliation
Capabilities

Every FLC workflow, covered.

From petition to paycheck, FieldPay handles the work that used to live across spreadsheets, paper timesheets, and multiple vendors or apps.

01

H-2A Compliance

Petition tracking, AEWR calculations across all operating states, primary duties test management, and audit-ready records.

02

Time & Attendance

Mobile clock-in for field crews, piece-rate capture, manual hour entry, and automatic merging with weekly payroll.

03

Payroll Engine

Weekly payroll runs, retro pay calculations, check generation in multiple stock formats, and positive pay file output.

04

Housing Registry

Property tracking, inspection logs, bed assignments with auto-population, and federal/state compliance documentation.

05

Farm Invoicing

All four billing models supported: percentage over payroll, hourly by headcount, per pound, and flat per-person daily rate.

06

Tax Compliance

Forms 940, 941, 943, and SSA EFW2 generation with H-2A exemption handling built in. State-by-state withholding rules.

07

Financial Automation

Multi-account bank reconciliation, automated daily transaction imports, recurring bill tracking, and farm P&L allocation.

08

Issue Tracking

Centralized inbox for payroll issues from the field — categorized, assigned, and resolved with a full audit trail.
